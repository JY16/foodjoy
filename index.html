About

FoodJoy is a local, family-owned cafe located in the Heart of North Carolina. Here at FoodJoy, we value our customers and our community. We strive to only use locally sourced ingredients whenever possible, however, some of our offerings require non-local ingredients. Whether our ingredients are local or non-local, they are of the finest quality.

FoodJoy provides:

  • Fresh, Quality Ingredients
  • Completely Made-From-Scratch Meals
  • Friendly, Comfortable Atmosphere

When you're at FoodJoy you're family. Even after you leave.
